AI Dubbing Tips (HeyGen / Rask AI):

1. Speaking pace: Speak 10-20% slower than normal. English tends to be longer than Vietnamese, so a slower pace helps lip-sync match better.

2. Microphone: Use an external mic or AirPods Pro. Cleaner audio means more accurate voice cloning.

3. Lighting: Ensure your face is clearly visible — lip-sync needs to see your mouth clearly.

4. Background music: Use only lofi/ambient at low volume (10-15%). No music with vocals.

5. Review the translation: After AI dubbing, check technical terms: escrow, ZK-SNARK, PLONK, blockchain, smart contract — make sure the AI translated them correctly.

6. Export: Export at 1080p or higher. Consider adding English subtitles for accessibility.
